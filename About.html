This is PETmalu!
As someone who has had a number of pets, and presently have two dogs, I believe that pets are one of the greatest mood-boosters and stress-reducers. While I do believe that everyone should consider owning one, not everyone is capable of doing so. I believe one of the most common mistakes people make when adopting a pet is that they don't conduct enough research beforehand on how to properly care for a pet, and the pet ends up neglected or mistreated. So, I decided to create this website as a guide on what to consider before adopting a pet so that all animals can live in a comfortable and loving home.		 About the Creator



Hello! I am Sofiya Anika Cueno from Batch 2025. I dedicate this website to my two dogs, Chewey and Crinks who have been my source of joy and inspired me to create this website. I also got inspired by various animal shelter organizations that dedicate their time and efforts to rescue and love the unloved animals.
Meaning behind PETmalu
The term petmalu is a syllabic reversal of the words malupit and malupet, which in Filipino language means anything that's exceedingly interesting. I chose the term petmalu since I wanted this website to be both informative and cool!
